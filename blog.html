Smart T-Shirt (For Blind and Deaf Children)

Published: 2021 • Project • Assistive Technology

The Smart T-Shirt improves communication for specially challenged children by displaying scrolling messages on an LED display (name, address, mobile). The shirt integrates GPS tracking paired with an Android application so parents can locate the child if necessary.

Key features: LED message display, GPS tracking, Android app integration, low-power microcontroller.

Why it matters: accessibility-first solutions reduce risk and improve independence for vulnerable children.

3D Mesh Based Enhanced Face Recognition

Published: 2023 • Research • Computer Vision

This system uses Face Mesh technology to recognize faces across different lighting and backgrounds. The research led to a survey publication: "Advancements in Face Recognition: A Survey of Methodologies in Computer Vision" (IJCRT).

Highlights: improved landmark localization, robustness to occlusion, and cross-demographic evaluation.
